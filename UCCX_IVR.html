IVR and Call Queuing

The section below describes the pre-provisioned IVR and how Agents are broken into CSQ's.

IVR

Dial 6000 from any Lab Phone and you'll hit our default IVR. Currently the IVR will collect the ANI and DNIS. This calling information is placed into Call Variables one and two respectivley. We have also set up the IVR to route to different CSQ's based on user input. How the call is routed has been listed below:
