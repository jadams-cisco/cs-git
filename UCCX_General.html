Welcome to the UCCX Sandbox!


The UCCX Sandbox is everything you need to develop against popular Finesse, UCCX, and SocialMiner API's! This Sandbox contains UCCX, Social Miner, CUCM 10.5, IM and Presence 10.5, CUC 10.0, MediaSense and a Domain Controller! Once you have reserved this lab, you'll have full administrative, API, and functional access to every server in the lab. It is YOUR environment!

How to connect to the lab:

1.) Press the 'Reserve' button at the top right

2.) Schedule a reservation up to one week

3.) At the reservation start time, check your email for VPN info

4.) If not installed already, download and install AnyConnect here!

5.) Using information from Email, connect to the VPN using AnyConnnect

6.) You may now connect to all servers directly, as your PC is on their local network

7.) Create, develop and test with no fear!
